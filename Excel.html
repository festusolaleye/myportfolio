Excel Projects

The project focuses on analyzing a sales dataset comprising 2000 records, each representing a single order with details such as Order ID, Date, Customer ID, Sales Person, and Item. Provided by Quantum Analytics NG, the dataset aims to offer insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends to aid in informed business decisions. To ensure data accuracy and reliability, thorough data inspection, correction of errors, and transformation were conducted.

Key insights from the analysis include total sales of GBP 2,028,591 from 9119 units sold across 2000 orders, reflecting robust commercial activity. Despite a modest customer base of 20, substantial sales volume and frequency suggest effective sales strategies. Laura emerges as the top revenue-generating salesperson, with Item 4 generating the highest revenue, indicating its popularity. Arizona leads in quantities of items sold, and May is the highest revenue-generating month.

Recommendations:
The success factors behind Laura's performance should be analyzed and implemented across the sales team to maximize revenue generation. Given the popularity of Item 4, ensure adequate stock levels to meet demand and capitalize on its profitability.

The dataset encompasses Foot Locker's retail sales data for 2020, comprising details like retailer ID, invoice date, region, product specifics, pricing, units sold, total sales, operating profit, margin, and sales method. Analyzing the data involves steps such as inspection, cleaning to rectify errors, transformation for analysis preparation and visualization to unveil sales trends and patterns.

Key Insights: Foot Locker's 2020 sales totaled GBP 717,821,450.00, with 2,016,512 units sold to 8,346 customers. The West region, especially Charleston, led in sales. In-store channels outperformed others. Men's apparel was the top-selling category. July was the most profitable month. Foot Locker emerged as the top retailer, capturing market share effectively.

Recommendations
Given the strong performance in the West region, allocating additional resources and marketing efforts to further capitalize on this lucrative market segment should be considered.
Targeted marketing campaigns should be tailored to Charleston to leverage its status as the top-performing city and potentially replicate its success in other high-potential cities.
